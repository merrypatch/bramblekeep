## How to sign

Contributions are gated by an automated CLA check on each pull request. When you open your first PR, a bot will comment with a link; sign in with your GitHub account and confirm once. Your signature covers all your future PRs to this repository.

The signature record is stored in this repository (see [`.github/workflows/cla.yml`](./.github/workflows/cla.yml)). No third-party service holds your data.
